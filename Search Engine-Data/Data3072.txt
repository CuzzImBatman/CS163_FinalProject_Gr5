Oxygen Not Included Wiki

Oxygen Not Included is an early alpha space-colony simulation video game developed by Klei Entertainment. The release date was February the 15th on Steam. Oxygen Not Included allows players to manage their Duplicants and help them dig, build and maintain a subterranean asteroid colony. Duplicants will need food, oxygen, sanitary and ambient temperatures to keep them alive, and even more than that to keep them happy.
