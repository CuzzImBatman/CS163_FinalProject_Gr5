Heat, Humidity on the Way for the Weekend
The weekend is expected to bring heat and humidity, according to KSTP Meteorologist Nicole Mitchell.Sunshine and temperatures in the mid to upper 80s are expected during the day Saturday, Mitchell said.Similar conditions are expected Sunday, with temperatures reaching the upper 80s. Mitchell added there is a chance for scattered storms and showers late in the day Sunday. After a front passes through the area, Mitchell said Monday and Tuesday are expected to be extremely comfortable with low humidity and temperatures in the low 80s.The sun shines over West Bush Lake Park in Bloomington.
